About this test

A typing test is a simple online tool that allows you to measure your typing speed and accuracy by testing how many words you can type in a given time. The test usually consists of a randomly generated passage that you have to type within a specific time frame, typically one to five minutes.

Taking a typing test is an excellent way to assess your typing skills and monitor your progress as you practice. It's a valuable tool for anyone who spends a lot of time typing, such as students, office workers, writers, and bloggers.

Typing tests can also help you identify specific areas where you need to focus your efforts to improve your typing skills.
